National English Hunt (NTH)

Creative writing is writing that make you express ideas and thoughts in a prolific way. Painting down your feelings and emotions is what creative writing does to you. We will be providing this opportunity to our students. A topic will be given and students will be asked to pour down their thoughts.


Syllabus



Class 1-


Articles, Unseen Passage (or Poetry), Jumbled letters and sentences, Masculine and Feminine, Nouns, Verbs, Singular and Plural, Animal- their Babies, sounds and Groups, Picture Identification, Simple Present Tense, Words- Meaning and Opposites, Ability to understand situation- bases variation in functions like thanking, greeting, asking for permission, introducing ( oneself) etc. Noun, Verbs, Conjunction and Punctuation, Spelling error, Preposition, Odd one out, Word and sentence completion.



Class 2-


Idioms and Phrases, Nouns, Verbs, Pronoun, Adverb, Adjective, Simple Present Tense, Conjunction, Preposition, Punctuation, Unseen Passage and Poetry, Word and Sentence completion, Odd one out, Singular and Plural, Masculine and Feminine, Picture description, Words- Meaning and Opposites, Jumbled letters and Sentences, Articles, Animals, Plants and Human Being Relationships, Dialogue Completion, Synonym and Antonym, Homophones.



Class 3


Homophones, Spelling error, Nouns, Pronoun, Verb, Adverb, Adjective, Simple and Past tense, Conjunction, Preposition, Punctuation, Articles, Picture description, Dialogue completion, Words- their meaning and opposites, Singular and Plural, Masculine and Feminine, Synonyms and Antonyms, Unseen Passage and Poetry, Jumbled letter and words, Spelling error, Word and Sentence completion, Odd one out.



Class 4


Idioms and Phrases, Noun, Adverb, Prepositions, Verb, Conjunction, Adjective, Pronoun, Subject and Predicate, Jumbled sentences and words, Picture description, Unseen Passage (or Poetry), Animal, Plants and Human being relationships, Simple Present and Past Tense, Dialogue completion, Punctuation, Articles, Subject and Predicate, Synonym and Antonyms, Masculine and Feminine, Error and correction, Homophones, .Words- meaning and opposites.



Class 5


Idioms and Phrases, Synonyms and Antonyms, Noun , Verb, Adverb, Adjective, Conjunction and Punctuations, Proposition, Sentence error, Unseen Passage (or Poetry), Tenses, Articles, Subject and Predicate, Understanding information through Picture or short messages or letter, Word pairs, Odd one out, Homophones, Spelling error, Match the following ( relationships- Animals, Plants, Human Beings), Singular and Plural, Articles, Error and correction, Dialogue completion, Value based and Situation based questions, Words- Meaning.



Class 6


Phrasal Verbs, Idioms, Verbs, Adverbs, Noun, Pronoun, Adjective, Tenses, Articles, Prepositions, Conjunctions, Punctuation, Unseen Passage or Poetry, Dialogue completion, Synonym and Antonym, Words-Meaning , Error and correction, Insert the correct word, Picture description, Subject and Predicate, Subject-Verb Agreement, Value based questions, Situation based questions, Collocation, Jumbled sentences and passage, Homophones and Homonyms, Modals.



Class 7


Error and correction, Modals, Phrasal Verb, Subject Verb Agreement, Idioms, Homonyms and Homophones, Noun, Pronoun, Verb, Adverb, Adjective, Tenses, Articles, Prepositions, Conjunctions, Unseen Passage or Poetry, Picture description, Dialogue completion, Synonym and Antonym, Word Meaning, Subject and Predicate, One word, Spelling, Voices and Narration, Collocation, Jumbled phrases.



Class 8


Analogies and Spellings, Reported speech, Synonyms, Antonyms, Subject Verb Agreement, Collocations, Modals, Phrasal Verb, Idioms or Proverbs, Error and correction, Word meaning, Prepositions, Conjunctions, Homonyms and Homophones, Noun, Pronoun, Verb, Adverb, Adjective, Conjunction, Prepositional phrases, Punctuation, Social cause, Jumbled Phrases, Tenses, Articles, Unseen Passage and Poetry, Dialogue completion, Determiners, Voices and Narration.



Class 9


Reported Speech, Modals, Subject Verb Agreement, Analogies and Spellings, Collocation, Phrasal Verb, Idioms or Proverbs, Error and Correction, Determiners, Tenses, Noun, Verb, Adjective, Conjunction, Punctuation, Prepositional Phrases, Voices Narration, Homonyms and Homophones, Unseen Passage or Poetry, Dialogue completion, Jumbled phrases, One word substitution, Rearrangements, Spotting error, Sentence completion, Conditionals.



Class 10


Synonyms and Antonyms, Analogies and Spellings, Reported speech, Modals, Subject Verb Agreement, Phrasal Verbs, Idioms or Proverbs, Error and Correction, Determiners, Tenses, Noun, Verb, Adjective, Conjunction, Punctuation, Prepositional Phrases, Voices Narration, Homonyms and Homophones, Unseen Passage or Poetry, Jumbled phrases, One word substitution, Rearrangements, Sentence completion, Conditionals, Concord, Word meaning.
