National Writing Hunt(NWH)- Creative writing is writing that make you express ideas and thoughts in a prolific way. Painting down your feelings and emotions is what creative writing does to you. We will be providing this opportunity to our students. A topic will be given and students will be asked to pour down their thoughts.

National Art Hunt(NAH)- Creative Drawing is a powerful exercise that will help students in thriving their mind skills. It will provide them an opportunity to articulate emotions and thought through figures and shapes.

National Maths Hunt(NMH)- It is a platform where students will get to enhance their Mathematics skill and Mental ability. The focus will be on mathematical reasoning, concepts, accuracy and speed.

National Science Hunt(NSH)- Conceptual knowledge and theoretical knowledge is what we will be perceiving through this Talent Hunt. It will give them a roller coaster ride in the world of Science.

National Vedic Maths Hunt(NVH): Vedic Mathematics can definitely solve mathematical numerical calculations in faster way. Some Vedic Math Scholars mentioned that Using Vedic Maths tricks you can do calculations 10-15 times faster than our usual methods. We aim to bring together the advantages of Vedic maths to hone the skills of students in mathematics.
